Let's Grow Together!

Do you want to grow together with people from different nations? You have come to the right place!

We are a Christian community with a heart for the nations. We will grow together and encourage one another. It's okay if you are still early in your spiritual journey; we will move forward together.

We will meet in a small group where we can interact with one another. We will use English in our meetings, but it's okay if you can't speak English fluently yet.

We will meet online every Sunday at 10 am WIB. There is more information below.
